REFLEXIVE AND EMPHATIC PRONOUNS

  • REFLEXIVE PRONOUNS
  • A reflexive pronoun is used as the direct object of a verb.
  • A reflexive pronoun is formed by adding '-self ' for the singular or '-selves' for the plural.

Examples:

1. Suddently, I found myself in a
dark alley.

2. The cat injured itself in the fight.

More examples:

  1. She hurt herself in the accident.
  2. The cat licked itself.
  3. They enjoyed themselves very much.
  4. We can look after ourselves.
  5. They have just bought themselves a new car.
