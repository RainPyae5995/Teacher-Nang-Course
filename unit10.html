INTERROGATIVE PRONOUNS

An interrogative pronoun is used to ask a question.

Read the following questions and answers.

1. What did you see?
    I saw a cow.

2. Who is that man?
    That man is our principal.

3. Whom did you give the money to?
    I gave the money to Mike.

4. Which of the two boys is Mary's brother?
    The taller boy is Mary's brother.
